This folder contains placeholder assets for the Space Invaders game.

Required files:
- player.png: Player ship sprite (64x32 or similar)
- alien.png: Alien invader sprite (48x32 or similar)
- bullet.png: Bullet sprite (8x24 or similar)
- barrier.png: Barrier block sprite (48x32 or similar)
- background.png: Background image (960x720 or scalable)
- bgm.mp3: Background music (loopable)
- shoot.wav: Player shooting sound
- explosion.wav: Alien explosion sound
- hit.wav: Barrier/player hit sound

You can replace these files with your own art and audio. Use PNG for images and MP3/WAV for audio.
